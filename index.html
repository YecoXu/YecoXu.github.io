Biography

I am currently a PhD student at the School of Computer and Information Studies, Hefei University of Technology. I received my Master's degree in Computer Technology from the College of Computer and Information of Hefei University of Technology in 2023. I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Hubei University in 2018.

My research interests include Mesh Feature Extraction, Surface Reconstruction from Point Cloud, and Quad Mesh Generation.
